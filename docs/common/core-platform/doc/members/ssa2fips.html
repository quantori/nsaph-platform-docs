The ssa2fips Module

Utility to create in-database mapping table between state and county codes used by SSA for Medicare claims and Federal Information Processing Series (FIPS) codes

Data has been taken from National Bureau of Economic Research

https://www.nber.org/research/data/ssa-federal-information-processing-series-fips-state-and-county-crosswalk
